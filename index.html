Totally is a 1/2 hour pilot created by Aleeza Marashlian. It's a portrait of a 12-year-old's life in suburban Los Angeles in the early 1990's contrasting the slow moving dusty sun of the place against the biting trivialities of quotidian Jr High life. This moody story of being a girl is captured with the sensibility of Life Magazine photo essays meets gum-popping Valley Girls.
